How to Order

Thank you for choosing to shop with us! Currently, we do not process online transactions or take online orders through our website. However, we are more than happy to assist you with your purchases in person or over the phone.

To place an order, please follow these steps:

1. Contact Us: Give us a call at +64 21 155 0537. Our friendly team is available to help you with any inquiries, provide product information, and take your order directly over the phone.

2. Visit Us: You are welcome to visit our store located at 25 Alexandra Street, Te Awamutu, New Zealand. Our knowledgeable staff will be delighted to assist you in finding exactly what you need.

We appreciate your understanding and look forward to serving you either in person or via phone. Thank you for your continued support!
