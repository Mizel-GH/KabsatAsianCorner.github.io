ABOUT US

OUR STORY

Our Family is proudly hailed from Abra, Philippines. KABSAT is a term from our Ilocano dialect which means "Siblings". Ilocanos are known for their warmth and hospitality, often welcoming guest and offering food and shelter.

Our logo speaks for our two sons as brothers/siblings. Hence, we at Kabsat Asian Corner, we treat not just our fellow compatriots (kababayans) but every customers as our Kabsat.

We aim to bring traditional Filipino and other Asian products the taste of home here in Te Awamutu.

KABSAT ASIAN CORNER is a family-owned Filipino grocery store in Te Awamutu, Waikato, New Zealand. The idea was born out of the need to help bring Filipino products to the Filipino community in Te Awamutu and its surrounding areas.

When our family moved to Te Awamutu back in 2019, I realized the need for having a Filipino grocery in town since we always had to travel to Hamilton to buy Filipino products. But it was not until May 2023 when I finally found the courage to pursue its establishment.

We initially started selling grocery products in our garage to our friends until I was able to secure a lease for the physical store. We opened our doors for the first time to the public on September 1, 2023.

Your ultimate hub for Filipino and Asian groceries awaits! Step into our store and find a wide array of fresh, authentic ingredients and products. Come shop with us and experience the taste of home!
